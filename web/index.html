Game Controls

1. Use the mouse to click and hold on a tan to take possession of it. Release to drop the tan.

2. Once you are holding onto the tan, you can drag it to a different place by moving the mouse.

3. Use 'x' while holding the tan to rotate it clockwise.

4. Use 'z' while holding the tan to rotate it counter-clockwise.

5. Have fun! :)
